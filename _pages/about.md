Hello! My name is Emmy, and I'm a current 4th year student at the University of Toronto, with a specialist in Computer Science and a major in Cognitive Science. I do research in computational cognitive science, mostly using the tools of computational linguistics/NLP. <!--I'm broadly interested in understanding how (pithy statement) -->

I've been fortunate to work with [Yang Xu](http://www.cs.toronto.edu/~yangxu/) at the Cognitive Lexicon Lab at UofT, as well as [Joseph Williams](http://www.josephjaywilliams.com/) at the Intelligent Adaptive Interventions Lab. I've also worked remotely with [MH Tessler](http://www.mit.edu/~tessler/) and [Roger Levy](http://www.mit.edu/~rplevy/) at the Computational Psycholinguistics Lab at MIT.  

I'm starting a PhD at Carnegie Mellon in Fall 2021, where I will be supervised by [Graham Neubig](http://www.phontron.com/).

I was also the Chief Editor of the Review of Undergraduate Computer Science ([RUCS](https://rucs.ca/)) from 2019-2020.  

In my free time, I like reading, trying new recipes, and taking long walks.
